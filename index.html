About Michael

Michael Larry Ancona is a 24-year old multimedia artist from Isulan, Sultan Kudarat is a passionate digital artist known for his minimalistic and dynamic pixel shading technique typically appears in video games. His previous art defines pixel art, based on a traditional 8-bit to 24-bit visual design that came around the 1950s era of video games. He believe that digital media art forms are the future of digital age. He spends his whole career developing and revolutionizing modern digital art and applying interactive experience in some of his works.
